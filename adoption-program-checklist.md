# AI Adoption Program Checklist

A quick checklist for planning an internal AI adoption program.

## 1. Objectives

- What are the 2–3 outcomes this program should achieve?
- Which teams or roles are in scope?
- How will success be measured?

## 2. Stakeholders

- Which leaders need to be informed, involved, or accountable?
- Who are the AI champions or early adopters?
- Who might be skeptical or concerned, and why?

## 3. Foundations

- Are basic guardrails and acceptable-use guidance in place?
- Are approved tools and access paths clear?
- Is there a simple way to ask questions or get help?

## 4. Enablement

- What training or workshops are needed first?
- What practical examples will you use (real work, not toy demos)?
- How will people practice safely on their own work?

## 5. Communication

- How will the program be announced and explained?
- How often will you share progress, wins, and learnings?
- How will feedback be collected and acted on?

## 6. Support and iteration

- How will champions be supported (time, recognition, resources)?
- How will you capture and share good patterns and prompts?
- When will the program be reviewed and adjusted?
