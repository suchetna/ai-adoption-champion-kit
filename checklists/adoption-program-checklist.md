# Adoption Program Checklist

Use this checklist to plan or review an internal AI adoption program.

## 1. Objectives
- Are the desired outcomes of the program clear?
- Are the target teams or roles defined?
- Is success measurable?

## 2. Stakeholders
- Are leaders aligned on the program?
- Are champions or early adopters identified?
- Are likely concerns or sources of resistance understood?

## 3. Foundations
- Are guardrails and acceptable-use guidance in place?
- Are approved tools and access paths clear?
- Do people know where to get help?

## 4. Enablement
- Is there practical training tied to real work?
- Are examples and use cases relevant to the teams involved?
- Can people practice safely?

## 5. Communication
- Is there a clear rollout message?
- Will progress and learnings be shared regularly?
- Is there a way to collect feedback?

## 6. Iteration
- Are adoption blockers being captured?
- Is there a plan to improve the program over time?
- Are useful patterns being documented and reused?
